What I do

Where I add value

01

UX Design & Facilitation

End-to-end experience design with a strong focus on user flows, interaction design and information architecture. I facilitate design thinking workshops and design crits - in person, remote and asynchronously - using tools like Loom to keep distributed teams moving.

02

UX Research

Mixed-method research across the full discovery-to-validation cycle. Methods include stakeholder and user interviews, contextual enquiry, usability testing (moderated and unmoderated), card sorting, journey mapping, task analysis and affinity mapping.

03

Cross-functional Collaboration

Comfortable across disciplines and with stakeholders at every level - PMs, BAs, engineers, commercial leads and clients. Experienced in requirements gathering, Agile and Waterfall delivery, and translating between design thinking and technical constraints.

04

Independent & Async Work

Experienced working independently, remotely and asynchronously across distributed teams. I've run design crits and design thinking workshops asynchronously - keeping decisions moving without requiring everyone in a room at the same time.

About

A bit about me

I'm TJ - a senior product designer and UX researcher with over 10 years' experience across finance, fintech, e-commerce, utilities, publishing and B2B SaaS. I work best on products where getting things right really matters - where clarity, trust and precision aren't optional extras.

I'm research-led by instinct. I start with discovery, think holistically and design experiences that hold up under pressure - not just in a prototype. I'm equally comfortable running a stakeholder workshop, facilitating a usability session, gathering requirements across functions, or getting into the detail of a user flow.

I've worked in-house, agency-side and as a freelance lead - in Agile and Waterfall environments - including as the first UX hire in a new Product team at Car & Classic, where I helped establish the design practice during a period of significant growth. I'm now focused on fintech and SaaS challenges, working fully remotely on a contract and freelance basis.

Outside of product work, I run Coastarian Creative - a vehicle for design, coaching and creative projects. I'm also developing an English course for non-native English speakers working in tech, alongside completing my UX writing qualification.
